Adithya Balachandran

In 2020, he was selected to the USA team for IOAA, and he earned a gold medal in the Global e-Competition in Astronomy and Astrophysics. Additionally, Adithya won the 2019 USA Junior Math Olympiad (USAJMO) and attended the Math Olympiad Program (MOP) in 2019. Adithya's favorite physics subject is thermodynamics. Other than physics and math, Adithya also enjoys coding and playing card games.
